Introduction & Background

The discovery of DNA's structure was monumental in the world of biology. Its discovery solved countless mysteries regarding protein synthesis and heredity. However, there are still mysteries of DNA that exist today. Over 99% of all human DNA is the same between individuals, yet there are still obvious biological differences between people. In this project we looked into genetic testing to se how DNA and these biological differences interact with race.
